I am a Portuguese architect and full stack developer now living in Stockholm. After highschool I decided I wanted to enroll in an architectural school and invest in my love for this field. Almost as soon as I finnished and presented my master theasis I moved from Lisbon to Stockholm with my boyfriend and started working as an architectural freelance here. However, last year I found a new passion: programming, and decided to change career. I still very much love architecture, but I simply discovered that I love programming more and so I enrolled in Code Institute's Full Stack diploma. Now, after finnishing the diploma I am super eager to start my new career and to keep learning and developing my programming skills.
Comming from an architectural background I am a very creative person with knowledge of design tools that can be relevant and a good asset in my future as a programmer. I am also a very social, dedicated and hard-working person, with a good sense a responsability and always willing, even eager, to learn more and more.
